Key Insights

Vaccine passports were rolled out without consensus on how to effectively utilise them. Varying pandemic management approaches have led to differences in countries' policies.

Big countries with regional governmental jurisdictions do not have federal vaccine passport policy. Regional authorities decide whether and how to impose vaccine passport requirements, e.g., Brazil, China and the United States.

A. International Vaccine Passport Schemes Providing valid vaccine passport to prove immunity status when traveling from one country to another.

B. Domestic Vaccine Passport Schemes Providing valid vaccine passport to prove immunity status when entering public spaces.
